Ink Tank

Ink Tank is a social app for tattoo lovers and tattoo artists. With Ink Tank, users can search for tattoo inspiration, share their favorite designs, and even schedule appointments with a tattoo artist near them.

For this project, I used Adobe XD, Photoshop, Illustrator, and After Effects. Check out the project page to learn more about my process.

View Ink Tank

WordStorm

WordStorm is a vocabulary learning app for people learning English or people who are looking to improve their skills. In WordStorm, people can test their knowledge daily, study with flashcards, and test their progress with quizzes.

For this project, I used pen and paper, Adobe Photoshop, and Prott. This was my first project, and taught me the foundations for good research and design.
